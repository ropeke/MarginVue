ABOUT

This project was created by Rotimi Opeke in the Spring of 2013 when he decided to take on a project to enhance his web-development skills. Combining his love for Computer Science and Finance, he decided to embark on a project that would actively help him visualize his own investment portfolio without logging into his brokerage account.
He hopes to continue to flesh out the website with more functionality in the coming weeks and over the summer. His biggest focus now is learning Server-Query Languages and database communication languages so that he can create functionality for a login for users and provide them necessary information about stocks in the portfolio both from a quantatative and an analytical standpoint.
